Location

Neos Marmaras

A gem on the Sithonian coast

Neos Marmaras is a charming harbour town on the Sithonian peninsula of Halkidiki. Azure waters stretch to the horizon from your balcony. Paradisos beach is just 900 m away, the marina and local tavernas are a short walk, and the pristine beaches of Toroni and Nikiti are 20 km.
