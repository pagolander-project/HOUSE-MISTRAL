2. What data we collect

This is a static website — we have no backend, no database, and we do not directly collect or store any personal data.

We use two third-party services that may process data on their own infrastructure:

  • Google Fonts — loads typefaces (Cormorant Garamond, DM Sans) from Google servers. Google may log your IP address as part of the font request. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Maps — an interactive map embedded in the page, loaded only after you click "Accept" on the cookie banner. If accepted, Google may set cookies and log your IP address and usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Cookies and local storage

We store a single item in your browser's localStorage:

  • hm_cookie_consent — records whether you accepted or declined Google Maps. This value stays in your browser only and is never sent to any server.

We use no tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics of any kind.

4. WhatsApp communications

When you contact us via WhatsApp, your name and message are processed by WhatsApp (Meta Platforms, Inc.) under their own privacy policy. We use your message only to respond to your enquiry and will not share it with third parties.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at housemistral@gmail.com.
